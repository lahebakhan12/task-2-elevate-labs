# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
To create a Power BI dashboard that tells a clear business story using sales and profit data.

## Tool Used

## Dashboard Overview
- Sales trend analysis by month
- Category-wise sales and profit comparison
- Impact of discounts on profitability
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, and Profit Margin
- Interactive filters for Region and Year

## Key Insights
- Technology is the top-performing category in both sales and profit
- Furniture generates lower profit compared to other categories
- Higher discounts often lead to reduced or negative profit
- Sales increase during year-end months due to seasonal demand
